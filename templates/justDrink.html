We’re always moving with the times – offering the latest products available in the coffee industry. The online tea and coffee store began its journey in 2022. 01.

Mocha
Cold coffee

Mocha refers to any mixture of coffee with chocolate flavoring, even as simple as some hot chocolate with shots of espresso blended together. A caffé mocha is basically a chocolate-flavored variant of a caffé latte so it’s also based on espresso and hot milk. The difference is that a mocha contains chocolate flavoring and sweetener.

02.

Pumpkin Spice Latte
Hot Drink

Why this one? Well, we’d be remiss if we didn’t pay homage to the season in some sort of way. Thus, fall dictates that pumpkin spice feature somewhere in the diet, at least once. A mixture of espresso, steamed milk, and their signature pumpkin sauce, gives the drinker their ultimate spicy fall experience with undertones of cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove. This is one of the aptest drinks to help us transition into the fall season properly.
